Reasons to Microchip Your Pet

A microchip is a tiny device which contains an ID number that can be registered with your contact information. A microchip can be scanned to obtain this information, so if your pet ever goes missing the rescuers can get in touch with you! It is imperative if you choose to microchip your animal, you MUST register their microchips and keep your contact information current.

Why is it important to microchip your pet?

  • Microchipped pets are as much as 50% more likely to be returned to their owners than those without a microchip
  • It is an ID that cannot be tampered with
  • Your pet cannot lose it like a collar with tags
  • Your personal information is private
  • It is an almost painless procedure for your pet
  • Less than 2 percent of lost cats that enter animal shelters are reunited with their families
